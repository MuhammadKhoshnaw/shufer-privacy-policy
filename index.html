Privacy Policy

Our app does not collect any personal data from users. The only information we collect is the vehicle plate number and registration ID, which is stored locally in the app to track vehicle violations and associated fees. We do not upload user data to our servers or share any user data with third parties.

We take the privacy of our users seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of the data we collect. We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services in our app.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or the data we collect, please get in touch with us at xoshnaw.soft@gmail.com.
